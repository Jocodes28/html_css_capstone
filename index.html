About Us


Our goal is to provide our customers with a sensory experience that goes far beyond just your average candle. A Dash of Luxe offers hand poured, all natural scents, aimed towards enhancing your moods, well being, and quality of life. Let us help you Ignite Your Vibe! Tap into your sultry side with Violet Merlot. Channel the feeling of warm tropical beaches with Santorini. Bring back those holiday feels with Santa's Cookies. At 48 scents and counting, we are the Fragrance Haus dedicated to building, improving, and offering sincere action to "pour" back into the community.

A year into our launch, we've not only tripled our sales, but expanded our inventory to incorporate wax melts, linen sprays, diffusers and vanity trays. Who doesn't love options!? We have and will continue to collaborate with other small business owners, and even participate in pop ups all over the city, such as the WBLS CIRCLE OF SISTERS Expo in 2019. I took matters into my own hands when I was dissatisfied with expensive, generic, and toxic home fragrances. Dissatisfied with patronizing businesses that are completely detached from the concerns of its consumers. It is our mission to continue to be fully dedicated to improving our livelihoods by pouring into ourselves and our community. Aiding in optimal mental and physical health.
